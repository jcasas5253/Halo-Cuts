Our Stylists

Halo Cuts & Style is a full-service salon located in beautiful Mission Viego, CA. We are dedicated to bringing you quality service and our friendly and experienced stylists always have your best in mind. Each stylists brings their own unique skills and techniques when working together with our clients.

The Salon

We believe in operating a sanitary establishment. All instruments are cleaned and sanitized in a germicide solution used by hospitals after each service. Files and buffers are disposed after each use. We adhere to strict sanitation practices to ensure the health and safety of our customers.
